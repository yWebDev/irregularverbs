How to play?

Goal

Match the correct forms (I, II, and III) of 50 irregular verbs as quickly as possible.

Drag and Drop or Click

Select the correct verb forms and place them in the appropriate boxes labeled I, II, and III.

Track Your Progress

Time Spent: A timer at the top shows how long you've been playing.
Your Score: Displays the number of correctly matched verbs so far.

Check Your Answers

Once you've filled in the forms, click the Check button or press Enter to see if your answers are correct.

Challenge Yourself

Aim to complete all 50 verbs in the shortest time possible!
